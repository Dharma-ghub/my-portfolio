Road trip to the Georgia Cyber Center - Augusta on April 3, 2025

Me and my team from Cyberarch had the incredible opportunity to visit the Georgia Cyber Center in Augusta. We were warmly welcomed by the staff and had a chance to observe their day-to-day operations, from the Security Operations Center (SOC) to the secured digital forensics team. It was truly an inspiring experience that highlighted the cutting-edge work being done at the Center.

I thank Georgia Cyber Innovation and Training Center and Cyberarch for this opportunity😄.
